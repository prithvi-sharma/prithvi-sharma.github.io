Lip Reading using Deep Learning

Lip Reading using Deep Learning

Lip Reading / Visual Speech Processing using Deep Learning (Final Year Project)

Created by:

Prithvi Sharma , Samarth Shahu, Pradyuman Giri, Aishwarya Poojarey

Completed on:

March, 2021

Skills:

Python / CNN / ReLu

Lip reading, otherwise known as Visual Speech Processing is a technique of perceiving spoken words by visually interpreting the movements of the lips, face, and tongue when actual sound isn't available.

In basic words, it is a visual method of ‘listening’ to someone. Often called ‘The third ear’ lip reading goes beyond simply reading the lips of a speaker to decipher individual words.

It also relies on information provided by the context, knowledge of the language, and any residual hearing additional to it for better subconscious analysis.
